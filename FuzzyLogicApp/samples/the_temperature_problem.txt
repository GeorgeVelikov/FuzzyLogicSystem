temperatureRulebase

Rule 1: If temperature is High or current is high then output is reduce
Rule 2: If temperature is Medium then output is same
Rule 3: If temperature is Low and current is high then output is same
Rule 4: If temperature is Low and current is low then output is increase


current

medium 10 10 10 10
high 20 20 10 0

temperature

Medium 200 200 150 150
High 400 450 150 0

output

reduce -50 -50 50 50
same 0 0 50 50
increase 50 50 50 50
